Crystal animals collector

Here is how you play the game!

  1. You will be given a random number at the start of the game."Number To Get"
  2. There are four different types of crystal animal below.
    • Green Froggy
    • Red Wolf
    • White Bunny
    • Purple Unicorn
  3. By clicking on a crystal animal, you will add a specific amount of points to your total score.
  4. You win the game by matching your total score to the random number.
  5. You lose the game if your total score goes above the random number.
  6. The value of each crystal animal is hidden from you until you click on it.
  7. Each time when the game starts, the game will change the values of each crystal animal.
